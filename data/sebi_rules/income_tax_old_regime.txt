Income Tax — Old Regime Tax Slabs FY 2026-27

1. OLD REGIME SLABS:
- ₹0 to ₹3,00,000: NIL (0%)
- ₹3,00,001 to ₹7,00,000: 5%
- ₹7,00,001 to ₹10,00,000: 10%
- ₹10,00,001 to ₹12,00,000: 15%
- ₹12,00,001 to ₹15,00,000: 20%
- Above ₹15,00,000: 30%

2. STANDARD DEDUCTION: ₹50,000

3. DEDUCTIONS AVAILABLE:
- Section 80C: ₹1,50,000 (ELSS, PPF, EPF, NSC, etc.)
- Section 80CCD(1B): ₹50,000 additional NPS
- Section 80D: Health insurance (₹25,000 self, ₹25,000/₹50,000 parents)
- Section 24(b): Home loan interest up to ₹2,00,000
- Section 80TTA: Savings account interest up to ₹10,000
- HRA Exemption: Minimum of (actual HRA, 50%/40% of salary, rent - 10% salary)

4. HEALTH & EDUCATION CESS: 4% on total tax amount

5. WHEN OLD REGIME IS BETTER:
- If total deductions exceed ₹3-4L
- If claiming HRA + home loan interest
- If maximizing 80C + 80D + 80CCD(1B)
- Typically better for higher income with full deduction utilization
