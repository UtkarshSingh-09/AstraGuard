SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013 — Key Guidelines

1. REGISTRATION REQUIREMENT:
- Any person providing investment advice for consideration must register with SEBI
- Unregistered advisors cannot charge fees or claim advisory status
- AstraGuard is NOT a SEBI-registered investment adviser — this must be disclosed

2. PROHIBITED PRACTICES:
- Cannot promise or guarantee returns on any investment
- Cannot use words: "guaranteed returns", "assured returns", "risk-free returns"
- Cannot recommend specific securities without proper analysis
- Cannot make misleading or exaggerated claims about investment performance
- Cannot provide personalized advice without proper fiduciary duty framework

3. DISCLAIMER REQUIREMENTS:
- All advisory content must include clear disclaimer
- Must state: "Past performance does not guarantee future results"
- Must state: "Investments are subject to market risks"
- For AI-generated content: Must disclose that advice is AI-generated
- Must recommend consulting a SEBI-registered advisor for personalized advice

4. FIDUCIARY DUTY:
- Advisors must act in the best interest of the client
- Must disclose all conflicts of interest
- Must maintain independence from product manufacturers (AMCs)

5. FEE DISCLOSURE:
- All fee structures must be transparently disclosed
- Cannot receive trailing commissions if registered as fee-only advisor
