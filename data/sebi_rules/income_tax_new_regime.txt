Income Tax — New Regime Tax Slabs FY 2026-27

1. NEW REGIME SLABS (Default regime from FY 2023-24):
- ₹0 to ₹4,00,000: NIL (0%)
- ₹4,00,001 to ₹8,00,000: 5%
- ₹8,00,001 to ₹12,00,000: 10%
- ₹12,00,001 to ₹16,00,000: 15%
- ₹16,00,001 to ₹20,00,000: 20%
- ₹20,00,001 to ₹24,00,000: 25%
- Above ₹24,00,000: 30%

2. STANDARD DEDUCTION: ₹75,000 (increased from ₹50,000 in Budget 2024)

3. DEDUCTIONS ALLOWED IN NEW REGIME:
- Standard deduction: ₹75,000
- Employer's NPS contribution under Section 80CCD(2): up to 14% of salary
- Agniveer contribution: Section 80CCH
- Family pension deduction: ₹15,000 or 1/3rd of pension
- NO 80C, 80D, HRA, 80TTA, 24(b) in new regime

4. REBATE UNDER SECTION 87A:
- Available if total income up to ₹7,00,000 (net of standard deduction)
- Full tax rebate — effective tax = ZERO for income up to ₹7.75L approx

5. HEALTH & EDUCATION CESS: 4% on total tax amount

6. WHEN NEW REGIME IS BETTER:
- If total deductions under old regime < ~₹3-4L
- If salary is high with no HRA/home loan benefit
- Salaried individuals with simple tax structure
